Overview

ChicTech is a full day event dedicated to introduce high schoolers to Computer Science by teaching them to make their own project to show off to friends and family. This year, we will be going through how to make your own website and data visualization using Github Pages, Jekyll, and d3.js! We will be having Professor Wade Fagen-Ulmschneider, the creator of many visualizations used by news organizations and the government, as a keynote speaker and teaching the data visualization portion.



Stay tuned for ChicTech 2021 applications!
